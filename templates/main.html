ABOUT HEART DISEASE PREDICTION SYSTEM

One of the complex tasks in healthcare industry is predicting of heart disease and it requires more experience and knowledge. Some of the ways of predicting heart diseases are ECG, stress test and heart MRI etc. Here the system uses 14 parameters for predicting heart disease that include blood pressure, cholesterol, chest pain and heart rate. These parameters are used to improve an accuracy level.
